Peanut Butter Fudge


Description:

This is the best recipe for creamy and delicious peanut butter fudge I have ever used. It is great for sharing at work.


Ingredients:

  • ½ cup butter
  • 1 (16 ounce) package brown sugar
  • ½ cup milk
  • ¾ cup peanut butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 3 ½ cups confectioners' sugar

Directions:

  1. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Stir in brown sugar and milk.
  3. Bring to a boil and boil for 2 minutes, stirring frequently.
  4. Remove from heat.
  5. Stir in peanut butter and vanilla.
  6. Pour over confectioners' sugar in a large mixing bowl. (Editors note: Or any sugar really)
  7. Beat until smooth; pour into an 8x8 inch dish.
  8. Chill until firm and cut into squares.
